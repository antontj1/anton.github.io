Nasional

All Programmes (Study Programs) at Astronomy Study Program FMIPA-ITB have received accreditation from the National Accreditation Board for Higher Education (Badan Akreditasi Nasional – Perguruan Tinggi (BAN-PT)).

BAN-PT is the only accreditation intitution that has been authorized form Ministry of Education, Research and Technology of the Republic of Indonesia in improving quality of higher education, introducing and disseminating “New Paradigms in Management of Higher Education”, and increasing relevance, academic atmosphere, institutional management, efficiency and sustainability of tertiary education.

(Wikipedia)



The detail of accreditation is listed below:

Programme Decree Year Accreditation Expired Status
Undergraduate 5127/SK/BAN-PT/Akred/S/XII/2017 2017 A 27-12-2022 Active
Master 1405/SK/BAN-PT/Akred/M/VII/2016 2016 A 29-07-2021 Active
Doctoral 1782/SK/BAN-PT/Akred/D/V/2019 2019 A 28-05-2024 Active

Internasional

In addition to BAN PT Accreditation, Undergraduate Programme in Astronomy is certified by ASIIN (Akkreditierungsagentur für Studiengänge der Ingenieurwissenschaften, der Informatik, der Naturwissenschaften und der Mathematics), an international accreditation institution from Germany, from 26 June 2015 until 30 September 2020.

Mathematics IA

MA1101

Credits: 4


Lecturer: Mathematics Study Program   		Real number system, Inequalities, Functions and Limits, Derivatives, Application of Derivatives, Integrals, Application of Integrals, Transcendental Functions.

Elementary Physics IA

FI1101

Credits: 4 [1]


Lecturer: Physics Study Program   		Mechanics, Mechanical Waves, Fluid (Static and Dynamic), Thermophysics (Kinetic Gas Theory and Thermodynamics).

General Chemistry IA

KI1101

Credits: 3 [1]


Lecturer: Chemistry Study Program   		Atoms, elements and compounds; Concepts of mole and stoichiometry; Reaction in aqueous solution; Oxidation-reduction reactions; Energy and chemical reaction; Thermodynamics; Theory of atoms based on quantum mechanics; Chemical bonding; Molecular structure; Properties of gases; Intermolecular force and properties of liquids and solids.

Indonesian Language: Scientific Writing

KU1011

Credits: 2


Lecturer: MKDU   		Students are going to learn about spelling, word formation, grammar, logic, definition construction, paragraphs construction, paper writing conventions, and scientific writing organization.

Introduction to Mathematics and Natural Sciences

KU1160

Credits: 2


Lecturer: Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences   		This course introduces essencial concepts in mathematics and natural sciences. This course also gives academic culture and the ways of thinking on the development of mathematics and natural sciences.

Introduction to Computation

KU1102

Credits: 3


Lecturer: Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences   		Engineering and design in society, engineer as a profession, aspects in engineering, key elements of engineering analysis, steps in solving problems, concept of energy, conversion and conservation, and some examples of engineering discipline as well as ethics in engineering.

Mathematics IIA

MA1201

Credits: 4


Lecturer: Mathematics Study Program   		Techniques of Integration, Indefinite Forms, Improper Integrals, Infinite Series, 2D and 3D Geometries, Derivatives in Rn, Double Integrals, Ordinary Differential Equations.

Elementary Physics IIA

FI1201

Credits: 4 [1]


Lecturer: Physics Study Program   		Electricity and Magnetism, Electromagnetic Waves, Modern Physics.

General Chemistry IIA

KI1201

Credits: 3 [1]


Lecturer: Chemistry Study Program   		Properties of Solution, Chemical Kinetics, Chemical Equilibrium, Acid-Base Equilibrium, Solubility Equilibrium, Electrochemistry, Organic Compunds, Biochemistry.

Introduction to Engineering and Design

KU1201

Credits: 3


Lecturer: Faculty of Arts And Designs   		Class orientation and team preparation, student team activities: problem definition and formulation, propose alternative solution and conceptual design, experiment/implementation of design solution, evaluation of design solution.

English

KU1024

Credits: 2


Lecturer: MKDU   		Train the students' critical thinking skills in reading activities and in writing in academic field.

Sports

Credits: 2
